About Me

As a full-stack software engineer and former Division 1 athlete, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and competitive drive to every project. With a BS in Computer Science from UC Davis, I specialize in creating responsive web applications using HTML, CSS, and modern web technologies. My journey from walk-on to Team Captain of the UC Davis Women's Tennis Team, where I earned a full scholarship, has instilled in me exceptional leadership and communication skills that I now apply to software development.

Currently working as an Undergraduate Assistant Tennis Coach while pursuing software engineering, I demonstrate my ability to manage complex projects, analyze challenges, and deliver results. Whether I'm developing machine learning models with Python or building responsive web applications, I approach each task with the same dedication that earned me recognition as an ITA Scholar Athlete. My experience across both technical and athletic domains has shaped me into a focused problem-solver who thrives in collaborative, fast-paced environments.
